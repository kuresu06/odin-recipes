Oatmeal

If you have never made oatmeal properly you have come to the wrong right place :))

Ingredients (for a hefty portion)

  • 3 cups of water
    • You can use milk, but it doesn't make that much of a difference in taste and is more tedious.
  • 1.5 cups of oatmeal
  • 1/2 tbs of salt
  • Optional
    • Sourcream
    • Jam
    • Peanut butter

Preparation

  1. Bring the water to a boil
  2. Pour in the oats
  3. And now for the most exciting part - wait for 10-15 minutes
    • If you see that there is barely any water left, that means that it's done.
  4. Serve and eat with one or more optional ingredients.
    • Note: if you eat without any of the optional ingredients I will judge you silently
